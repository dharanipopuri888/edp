EDLAPADU

  • Edlapadu is a Mandal in Guntur District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. Edlapadu Mandal Head Quarters is Edlapadu town . It belongs to Andhra region . It is located 28 KM towards west from District head quarters Guntur. 270 KM from State capital Hyderabad towards west .

  • Edlapadu Mandal is bounded by by Prathipadu Mandal towards East , Pedanandipadu Mandal towards East , Nadendla Mandal towards west , Phirangipuram Mandal towards North . Chilakaluripet City , Narasaraopet City , Guntur City , Sattenapalle City are the nearby Cities to Edlapadu.

  • Edlapadu consist of 25 Villages and 18 Panchayats . Marripalem is the smallest Village and Edlapadu is the biggest Village . It is in the 49 m elevation(altitude) . This Place is in the border of the Guntur District and Prakasam District. Prakasam District Yddana Pudi is South towards this place .

  • Guntur , Amaravathi , Vijayawada (Bezawada) , Nagarjunakonda (Nagarjuna Sagar Dam) , Machelipatnam are the near by Important tourist destinations to see.
